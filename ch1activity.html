Elevate Church

“For this reason also, God highly exalted Him, and bestowed on Him the name which is above every name…” Philippians 2:9

Elevate Church was established in January of 2020. A group of individuals decided to gather and form a local body of believers. They asked Bro. Dale Denning to be the first pastor of this exciting church.

We have several different days and times of worship, including:

  • Sunday mornings at 9:00
  • Sunday mornings at 10:30 (masks are required for this one)
  • Wednesday nights at 6:30
