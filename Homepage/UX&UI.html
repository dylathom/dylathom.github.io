Project Site Plan/Specification

The website elevatechurch.com is the website for Elevate Church in Milan. The website’s mission statement is to inform the public about the church itself and how the site visitor can become involved. The intended audience is Milan and the surrounding community, such as Bradford, Medina, and Humboldt. To measure the website’s success, I can check the site’s traffic and see how many people visited the website’s pages. To track user satisfaction, a pop-up can be included asking the user questions such as, “Are you finding what you need?” or, “How helpful is our website?” One issue in developing the website will be my own lack of experience, but another issue could be bringing attention to the website and getting people not involved in the church already to visit the site.
